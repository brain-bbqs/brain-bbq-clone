# 003 — Communication Cadence: In-site Forum

**Pillar:** Comms · **Status:** next

## Why
Consortium communication is scattered across email, Slack DMs, and hallway conversations. A first-class, in-site forum gives members one durable place to ask questions, air frustrations, and get help — including from an AI co-responder that can point to existing resources.

## What
- Members-only forum under `/forum` with threaded discussions, tags, and pins.
- Optional **AI co-responder**: when a new post lands, the agent drafts a suggested reply citing existing consortium resources (devices, grants, publications). The suggestion is visible to the poster and to admins; humans still ratify.
- Anonymized digest of forum themes flows into the monthly newsletter (Spec 001).

## Constitution alignment
- Member privacy: forum is member-only; digests are anonymized.
- Agent transparency: AI suggestions are clearly labeled and never auto-post as another user.
- Evidence-linked: AI replies cite the resources they draw from.

## Success criteria
- ≥ 30 active members posting per month.
- Median time-to-first-response < 24h (AI or human).
- ≥ 40% of AI suggestions are ratified by a human responder as useful.

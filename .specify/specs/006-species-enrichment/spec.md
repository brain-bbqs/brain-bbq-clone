# 006 — Species Enrichment

**Pillar:** Knowledge Graph · **Status:** later

## Why
BBQS is inherently cross-species, but the species surface today is thin — mostly a name and a project count. To support translational work, every species needs behavior repertoires, vocalization/sound libraries, ethograms, and pointers to open datasets.

## What
- Extended species profiles with: behavior ontology terms, sample vocalization audio, ethograms, linked datasets (DANDI, EMBER, OpenNeuro), and known cross-species analogues.
- A per-species discussion surface (feeds into Spec 003 forum tags).

## Constitution alignment
- Cross-species: this spec exists specifically to strengthen the cross-species principle.
- Evidence-linked: every behavior term cites its ontology entry.

## Success criteria
- Top 10 BBQS species have complete profiles.
- ≥ 5 documented cross-species behavior analogues.
